## 📊 **Quality Metrics Explained**

### **PSNR (Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio)**
- **Higher = Better** - More signal, less noise
- **Good Range** - 20-40 dB
- **Excellent** - 40+ dB

### **SSIM (Structural Similarity Index)**
- **Range** - 0 to 1
- **Good** - 0.7+
- **Excellent** - 0.9+

### **Enhancement Score**
- **Range** - 0 to 100%
- **Good** - 60%+
- **Excellent** - 80%+

### **Processing Time**
- **Fast** - <100ms
- **Good** - 100-500ms
- **Acceptable** - 500ms-1s
