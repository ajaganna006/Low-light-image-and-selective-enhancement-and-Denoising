4. **🎨 Bilateral Filter** - Edge-preserving smoothing
   - Keeps sharp edges while smoothing flat areas
   - Best for: Natural-looking smoothing

5. **🔧 NLM Denoising** - Non-Local Means algorithm
   - Advanced noise reduction using similar patches
   - Best for: Heavy noise removal

6. **⚡ Unsharp Mask** - Deblurring and sharpening
   - Enhances edges and details
   - Best for: Blurry images, detail enhancement
